Website Evaluations

Yale Art School

The website for the Yale School of art. While supposed to be an artistic site, it just makes it hard to read and even just look at.

  • Contrast: While the website has the text boxes in different colors in order to contrast, it does not really contrast from the background as a whole.
  • Repetition: The website uses multiple different text styles and sizes throughout the website giving it a sloppy look.
  • Alignment: The test is all aligned to a center, right side of the screen which is decent, the pictures are also aligned as such.
  • Proximity: The website keeps related things together therefore fuffilling the Proximity condition.
Gamestop

The website for the brand: Gamestop, a major video game retailer in the US.

  • Contrast: The contrast of white and red on black works well
  • Repetition: The website sticks to the same styles repetitively.
  • Alignment: the text is center aligned and works properly with the pictures.
  • Proximity: objects of the same nature are grouped together to form similar sections.
